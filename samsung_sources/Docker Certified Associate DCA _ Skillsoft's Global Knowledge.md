Docker Certified Associate DCA

Mirantis' Cloud Native and OpenStack offerings have always been specifically designed with certification in mind. We have a wide variety of courses to help you fill in any gaps you may have, ensuring you head into your certification exam with confidence.

The Docker Certified Associate (DCA) exam is designed to validate a candidate's Docker skillset with real world questions designed by experienced Docker practitioners.

What are the prerequisites to the DCA certification?

There are no formal prerequisites, but ideally candidates will have six to twelve months experience of working with Docker.

What are the required exams and recommended training ?

There is no formal training course recommended for this exam but Mirantis have produced a

free study guide to help candidates prepare for certification

https://training.mirantis.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Docker-Study-Guide\_v1.5-October.pdf

The Docker Certified Associate certification is valid for 2 years, applicants will be required to update their certification every two (2) years.

Remote monitoring on your Windows or Mac computer - available worldwide

Why being Docker DCA certified?

While the Docker Certified Associate certification is designed for enterprise practitioners using the Docker Enterprise Edition (EE) platform in production, but many topics covered in the certification exam are also applicable to the Docker Community Edition ( CE) free of charge.

Being Mirantis Docker Associate certified allows you to display your Docker skills with an official Docker ID

You'll get a digital certificate and can use the Docker Certified Associate logo

Any business or potential employer can check your Docker Certified status online

You'll get exclusive access to the Docker Certified professional network on LinkedIn and receive invitations to only certified events
