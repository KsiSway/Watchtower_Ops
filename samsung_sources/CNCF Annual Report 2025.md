Published: February 24, 2026

2025 marks ten years of progress for the CNCF and was a pinnacle year for the evolution of open source cloud native innovation. CNCF now hosts more than 230 projects with over 300,000 contributors worldwide, making it one of the largest open source organizations in the world.
