Tizen Forum and Docs Closure Notice

Apr 16, 2026 1 min read

Developer.tizen.org Closure

The Forum on the Developer.tizen.org page has been closed as of April 16, 2026. Thank you for using the Forum.

For any feedback or inquiries regarding Tizen, please leave them on the

Tizen GitHub,

https://github.com/Samsung/Tizen.Issues/issues

and our team will respond as quickly as possible.

Docs.tizen.org Closure

The Docs.tizen.org page has been closed as of April 16, 2026.

Tizen development documentation is now available on the

new Docs platform

https://samsungtizenos.com/docs/

, where you can access content with improved usability.

Thank you for your continued support.

The Tizen Team
