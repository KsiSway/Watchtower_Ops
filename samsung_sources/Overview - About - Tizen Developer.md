App Developers on Tizen OS

Developers who create apps for Tizen-based devices can find all the necessary resources in one place. While the existing tizen.org site focuses on open-source APIs, SamsungTizenOS.com also provides proprietary APIs, SDKs, and development guides developed Internally. This allows developers to access all the information they need in one single site.

Manufacturers Looking to Adopt Tizen OS

SamsungTizenOS.com introduces exclusive features and innovations for manufacturers who are looking to incorporate Tizen OS into their products. By highlighting unique strengths of Tizen OS, including AI capabilities, SmartThings integration, and other Services, this platform helps others better understand the broader product ecosystem.

Tizen OS plays a vital role in our ecosystem, powering various devices ranging from Smart TVs to multiple smart home appliances. However, information regarding Tizen OS was scattered across several domains, namely tizen.org and developer.samsung.com. Furthermore, Since Tizen is an open source project, tizen.org had limitations in providing information specialized Tizen OS features tailored for our products. This made it difficult for external developers and stakeholders to acquire such knowledge.

SamsungTizenOS.com is a site for developers and stakeholders of Tizen OS, which is embedded in our products. The platform contains vital information on Tizen-based products, development tools, and highlights OS features tailored specifically for Tizen-based products.
